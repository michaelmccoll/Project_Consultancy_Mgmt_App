About the app

This is a Consultancy Management App. That is designed to be used as an internal application and not externally facing, although it could be altered to be client facing.

The consultancy would have a number of 'Consultants' on its books, that 'Clients' would hire for a specific, distinct piece of work. This is managed within the app as 'Assignments'.

The app would also allow for statistics and MI page to be automatically produced. This would enable the consultancy management team to assign appropriate consultants to clients and oversee the consultancy performance.
